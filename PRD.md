# Product Requirements Document: InsightPulse

## Project Name

**InsightPulse — AI Customer Feedback Analyzer**

**Live App:** https://sense-feedback-flow.base44.app  
**Status:** MVP Prototype / Public Preview  
**Role:** Product Manager / AI Product Prototype Owner  

---

## 1. Overview

InsightPulse is an AI-powered customer feedback dashboard that helps product and engineering teams convert raw customer reviews into sentiment insights, product categories, urgent issue flags, and AI-generated product summaries.

The project was built as an AI-assisted B2B SaaS prototype to demonstrate Technical AI Product Management skills across product strategy, AI workflow design, feedback triage, dashboard analytics, and roadmap prioritization.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Product teams receive customer feedback from many sources, including reviews, surveys, support tickets, and user comments. Manually reading and tagging this feedback is slow, inconsistent, and makes it easy to miss urgent product issues.

Key problems:

- Feedback is hard to organize at scale.
- Product teams spend too much time manually reviewing comments.
- Critical bugs or performance complaints can be buried in general feedback.
- Engineering teams need faster visibility into urgent issues.
- Roadmap decisions need stronger customer-backed evidence.

---

## 3. Product Goal

The goal of InsightPulse is to help teams quickly understand customer feedback and turn it into product action.

The product supports:

- Faster feedback review
- Sentiment classification
- Category detection
- Urgent issue identification
- Product health visibility
- AI-generated dashboard summaries
- Better roadmap and backlog prioritization

---

## 4. Target Users

### Product Managers

Need to identify customer pain points, track sentiment, and prioritize roadmap decisions.

### Engineering Leads

Need to detect urgent bugs, crashes, broken flows, and performance-related complaints.

### Customer Success / Support Teams

Need to understand recurring customer issues and escalate them with clear evidence.

---

## 5. Core Features

### Feedback Dashboard

A centralized dashboard that displays customer feedback insights and product health signals.

Includes:

- Total reviews
- Sentiment breakdown
- Product categories
- Recent reviews
- Urgent issue indicators

### Review Submission

Users can submit customer feedback directly into the system.

Includes:

- Customer name
- Review text
- Star rating
- Review classification output

### Mock Data Import

Users can import sample reviews to quickly test the dashboard.

### Sentiment Classification

Reviews are classified as:

- Positive
- Neutral
- Negative

### Category Detection

Feedback is grouped into product categories such as:

- Bug
- Feature Request
- UI/UX
- Performance

### Urgent Issue Detection

The system flags high-severity feedback when users mention urgent terms such as crash, broken, failed, error, or major business impact.

### AI Dashboard Summary Agent

An AI agent reads the dashboard signals and generates a concise product summary, highlighting sentiment trends, urgent issues, and recommended focus areas.
